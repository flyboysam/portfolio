Advanced Wing Design Optimization

Developed an innovative wing design using computational fluid dynamics (CFD) to optimize lift-to-drag ratios. The project involved extensive wind tunnel testing and finite element analysis to ensure structural integrity while achieving significant improvements in fuel efficiency.

This project demonstrates expertise in aerodynamic optimization, combining theoretical analysis with practical validation through rigorous testing protocols.

Composite Material Structure Analysis

Designed and analyzed lightweight composite structures for aircraft applications, focusing on carbon fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP) materials. Utilized advanced FEA techniques to optimize layup sequences and minimize weight while maintaining structural strength requirements.

The project resulted in a 25% weight reduction compared to traditional aluminum structures, demonstrating the potential of advanced materials in aerospace applications.
